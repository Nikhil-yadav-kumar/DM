Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience in cybersecurity?
No prior experience is required! This workshop is designed for beginners and intermediate learners. We'll start with the basics and gradually move to more advanced topics.
Will I receive a certificate after completion?
Yes! All participants who complete the workshop will receive a digital certificate of completion that you can share on LinkedIn and add to your resume.
What if I miss the live session?
Don't worry! The workshop will be recorded and you'll have lifetime access to the recording. However, we highly recommend attending live for the interactive experience.
What software or tools do I need?
You'll need a computer with internet connection. We'll provide detailed setup instructions via email before the workshop. All tools we use are free and open-source.
Is the fee refundable?
Yes, we offer a 100% refund if you request cancellation at least 48 hours before the workshop. No questions asked!
Will there be hands-on practice?
Absolutely! The workshop includes multiple hands-on labs and real-world scenarios. You'll get practical experience with security tools and techniques.
